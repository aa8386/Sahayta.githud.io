What we do

We design and implement agent-based and direct-to-beneficiary welfare scheme linkage programs for corporates, NGOs, philanthropies and government departments for the people.

Process to follow

Step 1

SAHAYTA trains citizens on its mobile app through a user manual. This helps to discover eligibility for welfare schemes using the app.

Step 2

Once the eligibility check is completed, the application provides detailed information about welfare schemes and documents to the citizen.

Step 3

Next, the application provides end-to-end application support including document checks, filling forms and submission of the application to relevant departments.

Step 4

Sahayta tracks the application till the citizen receives the benefits. Grievance redressals processes are initiated in case of delays

Step 5

The agents working under Sahayta earn a good amount of money and also become helpers for community.
